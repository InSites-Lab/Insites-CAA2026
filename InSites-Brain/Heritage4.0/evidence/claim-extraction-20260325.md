## D. Boundary Decisions Log

```
Decision B001: C006 — cupmarks "likely contemporary with dolmen construction as part of funerary ritual"
Counted as 1 claim (not split into "cupmarks are contemporary" + "they are funerary ritual").
Reason: The "as part of" makes the ritual interpretation dependent on the dating — it's a single inference chain.

Decision B002: C012 — "densest and best-preserved dolmen concentrations"
```
